Learn more about MJ Foundation

The MJ Foundation was founded in 2018 by Florence Marjana Ali with a mission to empower youth to realize their potential. The primary medium in which the MJ Foundation works is through education. The MJ Foundation has supported the work of its phenomenal partners to create pathways for the most vulnerable in society to attain and capitalize on education.
